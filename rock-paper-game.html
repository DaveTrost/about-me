Rock - Paper - Scissors

Game Play

Click on one of the left-side icons to throw. The computer will play against you and the computer always plays randomly.

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Paper beats Rock
  • Scissors beats Paper

This game has a betting feature which can be enabled or disabled using the checkbox on the right. When betting is enabled, each player will start with chips in their stack. Set your bet amount before making your throw. The game will end when one player has run out of chips.
